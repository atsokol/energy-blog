# Energy Analysis

Interactive analysis of electricity and gas markets in Ukraine and neighbouring EU countries.
Data is updated automatically from public sources (ENTSO-E, Market Operator JSC, Ukrenergo).

## Posts

- [**Economics of energy storage in Ukraine**](./storage) — Intraday price volatility, DAM and balancing market arbitrage opportunities, and how storage value in Ukraine compares to neighbouring EU markets.

- [**Renewables price capture in Ukraine and neighbouring EU markets**](./capture) — How solar and wind capture factors have evolved as renewable penetration rises, and what the cannibalisation effect means for project revenues.
