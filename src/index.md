# Infra & Energy Data Blog

<br>

### About me

Hi, I'm Andrii. By day, an infrastructure banker doing project finance across emerging markets. By inclination, a data analytics enthusiast pulling apart market data to see what's really going on.

This blog is where those two worlds meet: the practitioner's perspective and the curiosity of someone who enjoys turning public datasets into something visual and, hopefully, useful.

[Find me on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrii-tsokol-9626937/).

<br>

### This blog

A few principles I try to stick to:
- **Open data & open code** — relying on publicly available data and sharing the code behind the analysis
- **More charts, less text** — visuals first
- **Live dashboards** — data and insights kept current and reproducible

All views and errors are my own — traceable and challengeable.
